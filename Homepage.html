About

Students.
Engineers.
Racers.

We are a multidisciplinary team of students from TU Hamburg who design and build formula-style race cars from scratch every year. We compete at international Formula Student events including Formula Student Germany, Formula Student Netherlands, and others. Our team spans mechanical, electrical, software, and business disciplines — united by a passion for motorsport engineering and pushing the limits of what students can achieve.
